Our Services

We Are Providing Top Services With Excellent Performance

Civil litigation

Mutete John & Company Advocates litigation services both broad and deep, our Litigation & Dispute Resolution practice form the core of our services to our clients. Focusing on disputes that either reach the High Court or are resolved by arbitration, we are involved in many high-profile commercial litigation and arbitration matters in Kenya. Geared for rapid response, the Litigation & Dispute Resolution team can be called upon at short notice to resolve largescale and complex litigation matters. This includes urgent applications and discovery-driven trial and arbitration matters.

Family Law

Family is the basic social unit which is vitally important and which brings with it important legal concerns. Many family related matters have significant legal implications and Mutete John & Company Advocates seeks to help families deal with their issues well. We help families deal with legal matters ranging from marriage, adoption, divorce, reconciliation, succession, domestic violence and estate planning among other issues.We realize that dealing with family legal concerns is a very human undertaking requiring not just legal excellence, but also great care and compassion. If you or your families have a legal concern, please get in touch and let us find the best way to handle it.

Commercial Law

Commercial and corporate Law are at the heart of the firm. Through vast experiences from the individual partners and associates, the firm has with time developed great knowledge in this field. The Firm has detailed knowledge of local practices concerning licensing, permits, regulatory supervision, local shareholder participation requirements and cross-border due diligence in sectors as diverse as energy, mining, telecoms, fast-moving consumer goods and agribusiness.
